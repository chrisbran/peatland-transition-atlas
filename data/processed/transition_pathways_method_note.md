# Transition Pathways Dataset

This dataset translates the curated peatland rewetting literature into a qualitative visualisation layer for the Peatland Transition Atlas.

The pathway scores are not statistical estimates. They are transparent, literature-informed expert codings for prototyping:
- 1 = low
- 2 = medium
- 3 = high
- 0 = not applicable / reference state

The purpose is to visualise trade-offs between GHG mitigation, water-table requirement, trafficability, farm compatibility, market maturity, adoption barriers and transfer relevance for South Germany.

The `paper_ids` field links to `papers.csv`.
