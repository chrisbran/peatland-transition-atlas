# Region Case Studies Layer

`region_case_studies.geojson` is a visual evidence layer for the Peatland Transition Atlas.

Important caution:
- Points are approximate display anchors.
- Several records represent national, regional, review, policy or conceptual clusters rather than exact field coordinates.
- The layer should be used for storytelling and filtering international evidence, not for precise spatial inference.

The layer is derived from:
- `papers.csv`
- `transition_pathways.csv`
- manual literature coding of study region, theme, pathway relevance and South Germany transfer relevance.
